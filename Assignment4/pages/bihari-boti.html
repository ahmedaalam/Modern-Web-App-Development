Bihari Boti

Our Bihari Boti is a traditional BBQ favorite made from tender chunks of beef marinated in a rich blend of yogurt, raw papaya, mustard oil, and signature Bihari spices. Grilled over charcoal to achieve a perfect smoky aroma, each piece is juicy, flavorful, and melts in your mouth. A true taste of authentic Pakistani street-style barbecue with bold, rustic flavors in every bite.
