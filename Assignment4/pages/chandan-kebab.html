Chandan Kebab

Our Chandan Kebab is a creamy and mildly spiced delicacy made from tender minced chicken blended with rich cream, cheese, and aromatic herbs. Grilled to perfection, it offers a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture with a subtle smoky flavor. Perfectly balanced and irresistibly smooth, it’s a royal twist on traditional kebabs that’s both elegant and flavorful.
