Reshmi Kebab

Our Reshmi Kebab is a rich, creamy delight made from tender chicken marinated in yogurt, cream, and a blend of mild spices. Grilled to perfection, it delivers a silky, melt-in-your-mouth texture with a delicate smoky aroma. Perfectly balanced and full of flavor, this kebab is a true Mughlai-inspired classic that embodies softness and elegance in every bite.
