Malai Boti

Our Malai Boti is a creamy and tender delicacy made from boneless chicken pieces marinated in a rich blend of fresh cream, yogurt, cheese, and mild spices. Grilled to golden perfection, it offers a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture with a subtle smoky flavor. Perfectly balanced and irresistibly rich, this dish brings a royal, velvety touch to traditional BBQ.
