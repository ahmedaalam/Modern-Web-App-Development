Chicken Birynai

A fragrant and flavorful South Asian rice dish made with tender chicken pieces marinated in aromatic spices, layered with basmati rice, and slow-cooked to perfection. Each bite bursts with rich flavors of saffron, caramelized onions, fresh herbs, and a blend of traditional biryani masala. Served hot with raita or salad, this dish is a true comfort classic
